For all the third culture kids around the world, this is for you. As those who tend to struggle to find a sense of cultural identity, this website allows you to create a sense of belonging within culture through design. These patterns that are tailored and unique to your life experience can be transformed into homeware, or an object you use in your daily life that you can showcase your new pattern. After creating your pattern you can see some examples of products people have chosen to print their pattern on below.
